Project-2

Cryptography and Steganography Techniques:

In this project, I have proposed a new image Steganography technique by combining it with cryptography to provide great level of security. I implemented this new algorithm using MATLAB as a stimulator, this is based on LSB.I have used MatLab(2008) as a tool for implementing rail fence cipher and LSB approach for the work.In this secret message is inserted into the cover image by using the LSB based technique, then stego image is obtained and then we will analyze the result. The result shows increased security, increased Peak Signal to Noice Ratio (PSNR) and low Mean Square Error (MSE) and the quality of stego-image is also good.
